Inside the Arena

WCFC is hosted by Twitch Streamer: MelonCraft80 at twitch.tv/meloncraft80! WCFC Champions will enter the arena facing a single opponent. They have one chance to defeat them, until the next week of competition.

Over the course of the several weeks of the FightClub Season, Champions have the chance to compete in 1v1 and 2v2 rounds of competition (the rules of which can be found at Rules). WCFC Champions will rack up scores by competing in these weekly competitions to have a shot at the final competition, where the best of the best will compete for 15stx of LE.
